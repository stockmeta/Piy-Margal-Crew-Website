About Piy Margal

The crew mainly consists of only 3 members Me (Joshua), Rommel and Aris Laman. We mainly spot at P. Florentino Street due to its smooth and somehow flat surface. Due to heavy traffic and left and right vehicles crossing through, we now reside at Piy Margal corner M. Dela Fuente. But sadly, last December of 2017,
due to the accident that happened to an unknown skater. We mainly see them at the end of the street of M. Dela Fuente corner España they mainly spot)
at Fajardo St. but I heard they got kicked out due to negligence.

We are now growing but we skate less than Piy Margal days. We are now venturing on streets and sometimes we spot at Piy Margal, P. Florentino, Mendiola and KKK.

Update: finally, we are now allowed (somehow) to spot at our OG spot. But we are very careful if the chairman arrived unnoticed.
